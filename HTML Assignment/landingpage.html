Summary: Latitude vs.


The purpose of this project was to analyze how weather changes as you get closer to the equator. Leveraged data from the OpenWeatherMap API to assemble a dataset of over 500 cities.

After data parcing, used MatplotLib to plot various aspects of the weather vs latitude. Factors we looked at included: temperature, cloudiness, humidity, and wind speed. I give a little deep dive into the analysis so explore the site at your leisure!
